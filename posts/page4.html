151. Ohio Manufacturers’ Association challenges new utility billing for data centers

Factor This™ | Thu, 13 Nov 2025 18:58:17 +0000

The Ohio Manufacturers’ Association seeks to reverse a state supreme court decision allowing American Electric Power to charge data centers differently.

The Ohio Manufacturers’ Association has filed a challenge against new utility billing practices for data centers, arguing that the changes could lead to increased costs for manufacturers. The association claims that the new billing structure is unfair and could hinder the growth of the data center industry in Ohio.
