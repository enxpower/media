151. Feds release draft long-term plans for Colorado River management

Factor This™ | Mon, 12 Jan 2026 20:01:20 +0000

The Bureau of Reclamation released proposals for managing Colorado River reservoirs amid stalled negotiations among seven states over water sharing.

The federal government has released draft long-term plans for managing the Colorado River, addressing the ongoing water crisis affecting the region. These plans aim to balance water usage among the seven states that rely on the river, as well as Mexico, amid declining water levels and increasing demand.
