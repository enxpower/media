151. Kinneytown Dam, a major impediment to fish, could soon be removed

Factor This™ | Mon, 01 Dec 2025 19:56:51 +0000

The Connecticut Brownfield Land Bank purchased the Kinneytown Dam for $1, initiating a $60 million project to remove it and restore the Naugatuck River.

The Kinneytown Dam, located on the Housatonic River in Connecticut, is recognized as a significant barrier to fish migration. Plans are underway for its removal, which could enhance the river's ecosystem and improve fish passage.
