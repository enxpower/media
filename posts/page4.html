151. FERC should side with Voltus and Mission:data because residential demand response is blocked at PJM.

Factor This™ | Thu, 20 Nov 2025 18:42:46 +0000

Voltus and Mission:data filed a complaint at FERC against eight PJM utilities for blocking residential demand response by not sharing interval data.

The article argues that the Federal Energy Regulatory Commission (FERC) should support Voltus and Mission:data in their efforts to promote residential demand response, which is currently hindered at the PJM Interconnection. The blockage of residential demand response limits the ability of consumers to participate in energy markets and manage their energy usage effectively.
