151. Can purpose-built battery systems alleviate data center interconnection woes?

Factor This™ | Thu, 23 Oct 2025 17:45:36 +0000

Aligned Data Centers and Calibrant Energy announced a 31 MW battery energy storage system at Aligned’s Pacific Northwest campus, operational by 2026, to accelerate grid interconnection.

Purpose-built battery systems are being explored as a solution to alleviate interconnection issues faced by data centers. These systems can provide reliable energy storage, helping to manage the fluctuating energy demands and improve the overall efficiency of data center operations.
