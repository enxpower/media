151. Why energizing EV chargers takes so long—and what we’ve learned about fixing it

Factor This™ | Thu, 18 Dec 2025 16:46:07 +0000

IREC has assessed a set of regulatory solutions designed to improve the process of connecting EVSE to the grid in ways that align with customer needs, writes contributor Catelyn Sweeney.

The process of energizing electric vehicle (EV) chargers can be lengthy due to various factors including permitting delays, infrastructure challenges, and the complexity of electrical installations. Solutions to expedite this process have been identified, such as streamlining permitting and improving coordination among stakeholders. These improvements aim to enhance the deployment speed of EV charging stations.
