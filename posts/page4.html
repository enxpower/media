151. Feds release draft long-term plans for Colorado River management

Factor This™ | Mon, 12 Jan 2026 20:01:20 +0000

The Bureau of Reclamation released proposals for managing Colorado River reservoirs amid stalled negotiations among seven states over water sharing.

The federal government has released draft long-term plans for managing the Colorado River, focusing on balancing water supply and demand amid ongoing drought conditions. The plans aim to address the challenges faced by the seven states that rely on the river, including California, Arizona, and Nevada, as well as Mexico.
