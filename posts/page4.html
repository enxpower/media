151. Four governors whose states rely on PJM want data centers to guarantee their own power

Factor This™ | Thu, 30 Oct 2025 17:44:45 +0000

Four governors from Pennsylvania, Maryland, New Jersey, and Virginia proposed a fast-track approval process for data centers generating power for PJM Interconnection's grid.

Four governors from states that depend on the PJM Interconnection are advocating for data centers to ensure their own power supply. This push comes as these states seek to enhance energy reliability and manage the increasing demand from data centers.
