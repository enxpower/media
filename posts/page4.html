151. NHA hails DOE move to shield hydropower permits

Factor This™ | Mon, 17 Nov 2025 15:43:36 +0000

National Hydropower Association President Malcolm Woolf issues strong support for Energy Secretary Chris Wright's directive to streamline hydropower licensing with FERC.

The National Hydropower Association (NHA) has praised the Department of Energy's (DOE) decision to protect hydropower permits. This move is seen as a significant step in supporting the hydropower industry and ensuring the continued development of renewable energy resources.
