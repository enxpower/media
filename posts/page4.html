151. Renewable jobs lagging behind clean energy deployment

Factor This™ | Thu, 15 Jan 2026 16:07:00 +0000

A report from International Labor Organization and IRENA says the ‘human side’ of the energy transition is being overlooked.

Despite the rapid deployment of clean energy technologies, job growth in the renewable energy sector is not keeping pace. The article highlights that while investments in renewable energy are increasing, the number of jobs created is lagging, raising concerns about the sustainability of this growth.
