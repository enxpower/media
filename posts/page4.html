151. Understanding the benefits of Salt River Project’s shift from webTrans to webTrans AFC with Derek Sicher

Factor This™ | Mon, 22 Dec 2025 19:17:29 +0000

At the 2025 OATI Energy Conference, Derek Sicher, Senior Project Manager – IT at Salt River Project (SRP) sat down with John Engel from OATI to discuss ever evolving customer needs and how SRP is managing congestion and the transmission system.

Salt River Project (SRP) is transitioning from its existing webTrans platform to the new webTrans AFC system, which aims to enhance operational efficiency and data management. Derek Sicher discusses the benefits of this shift, including improved analytics and better integration of renewable energy sources into the grid. The upgrade is part of SRP's broader efforts to modernize its infrastructure and support sustainable energy initiatives.
