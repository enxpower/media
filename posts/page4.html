151. SECC and PLMA partner up to put customers at the center of utility DER strategies

Factor This™ | Mon, 20 Oct 2025 14:13:16 +0000

PLMA and SECC are newly collaborating to offer a combined one-day event focused on the critical intersection of customer engagement and distributed energy resources (DERs).

The Smart Electric Power Alliance (SECC) and the Peak Load Management Alliance (PLMA) have announced a partnership aimed at enhancing utility Distributed Energy Resource (DER) strategies by focusing on customer needs. This collaboration seeks to improve the integration of DERs into utility operations, ensuring that customer preferences and participation are prioritized in energy management.
