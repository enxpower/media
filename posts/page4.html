151. Renewable jobs lagging behind clean energy deployment

Factor This™ | Thu, 15 Jan 2026 16:07:00 +0000

A report from International Labor Organization and IRENA says the ‘human side’ of the energy transition is being overlooked.

A recent report indicates that job growth in the renewable energy sector is not keeping pace with the rapid deployment of clean energy technologies. While investments in renewable energy are increasing, the number of jobs created is lagging, raising concerns about the industry's ability to support a sustainable workforce.
