151. Bangladesh’s accession to the UN Water Convention has a ripple effect that could cause problems with India

Factor This™ | Mon, 03 Nov 2025 20:14:12 +0000

Bangladesh joined the U.N. Water Convention to manage transboundary waterways, but this may heighten tensions with India, experts say.

Bangladesh's recent accession to the UN Water Convention may lead to tensions with India, as it could affect water-sharing agreements between the two countries. The convention aims to promote the sustainable management of transboundary water resources, which could challenge existing arrangements in the region.
