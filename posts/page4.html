151. Eastern Shore group appeals Conowingo Dam certification

Factor This™ | Mon, 15 Dec 2025 20:40:01 +0000

A coalition from two Eastern Shore counties appealed Maryland's water quality certification for the Conowingo Dam, risking a $340 million settlement.

An Eastern Shore group has filed an appeal against the certification of the Conowingo Dam, arguing that the certification process did not adequately consider environmental impacts. The appeal highlights concerns over water quality and the dam's effects on local ecosystems.
