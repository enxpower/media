151. One of UK’s oldest hydropower plants to be upgraded by Voith’s Green Highland

Factor This™ | Mon, 20 Oct 2025 13:36:00 +0000

Green Highland has signed an agreement to provide full operational support and maintenance services at the Kinlochleven hydropower plant.

Voith's Green Highland is set to upgrade one of the UK's oldest hydropower plants, enhancing its efficiency and sustainability. This project aims to modernize the facility while preserving its historical significance in the renewable energy sector.
