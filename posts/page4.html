151. Kinneytown Dam, a major impediment to fish, could soon be removed

Factor This™ | Mon, 01 Dec 2025 19:56:51 +0000

The Connecticut Brownfield Land Bank purchased the Kinneytown Dam for $1, initiating a $60 million project to remove it and restore the Naugatuck River.

The Kinneytown Dam, located on the Housatonic River in Connecticut, is facing potential removal due to its significant obstruction to fish migration. The dam has been identified as a major barrier for aquatic life, and efforts are underway to assess its impact and explore removal options.
