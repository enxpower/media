151. As AI and data center power demand skyrockets, flexible load integration becomes a critical strategy for utilities

Factor This™ | Thu, 11 Dec 2025 17:13:43 +0000

The power requirements of AI and data are placing increasing pressure on power infrastructure, creating significant challenges for grid operators, utilities and developers alike, writes contributor Dr. Matt Green.

As the demand for power from AI and data centers increases significantly, utilities are turning to flexible load integration as a vital strategy. This approach allows for better management of energy resources and helps balance supply and demand in the grid.
