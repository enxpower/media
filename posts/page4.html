151. How to get Georgia voters to turn out for the PSC election? Talk about their power bills.

Factor This™ | Thu, 18 Sep 2025 18:16:13 +0000

Campaigns and activists are racing to educate voters on the Public Service Commission, elected officials with power over energy bills and climate action.

In Georgia, the upcoming Public Service Commission (PSC) election is being linked to voters' concerns about their power bills. Strategies to increase voter turnout include discussing the impact of energy costs on households and emphasizing the importance of the PSC in regulating these expenses.
