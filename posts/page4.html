151. How are DER flexibility markets transforming the grid and enabling utility savings?

Factor This™ | Mon, 15 Dec 2025 20:50:45 +0000

Transforming the grid to meet skyrocketing energy demand isn't just about switching generation sources.

Distributed Energy Resource (DER) flexibility markets are reshaping the power grid by allowing utilities to better manage demand and supply fluctuations. These markets enable utilities to tap into a variety of resources, leading to potential savings and improved grid reliability.
