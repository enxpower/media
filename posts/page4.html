151. What to know about COP30, this year’s UN climate talks

Factor This™ | Mon, 10 Nov 2025 22:00:00 +0000

Delegates gather in Belem, Brazil, for COP30, aiming to address climate change amid concerns over participation and outcomes.

COP30, the 2023 UN climate talks, will take place in Dubai from November 30 to December 12. The conference aims to address global climate change issues and advance international cooperation on climate action. Key topics include emissions reduction, climate finance, and adaptation strategies for vulnerable communities.
