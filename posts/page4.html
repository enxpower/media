151. Can these senators save NEPA? – This Week in Cleantech

Factor This™ | Fri, 12 Dec 2025 16:37:55 +0000

On this episode of the This Week in Cleantech podcast, Jael Holzman from Heatmap News discusses how several senators came out against the SPEED Act, a bill that would change the National Environmental Policy Act, or NEPA.

Senators are discussing potential reforms to the National Environmental Policy Act (NEPA) to streamline the permitting process for clean energy projects. The aim is to balance environmental protections with the need for faster deployment of renewable energy solutions.
