151. Understanding the benefits of Salt River Project’s shift from webTrans to webTrans AFC with Derek Sicher

Factor This™ | Mon, 22 Dec 2025 19:17:29 +0000

At the 2025 OATI Energy Conference, Derek Sicher, Senior Project Manager – IT at Salt River Project (SRP) sat down with John Engel from OATI to discuss ever evolving customer needs and how SRP is managing congestion and the transmission system.

Salt River Project (SRP) is transitioning from its existing webTrans system to the new webTrans AFC platform, which is expected to enhance operational efficiency and improve data management. Derek Sicher discusses the benefits of this shift, including better integration of renewable energy sources and more effective grid management. The upgrade aims to support SRP's commitment to modernizing the power grid and optimizing resource allocation.
