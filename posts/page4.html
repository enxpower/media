151. Ohio Manufacturers’ Association challenges new utility billing for data centers

Factor This™ | Thu, 13 Nov 2025 18:58:17 +0000

The Ohio Manufacturers’ Association seeks to reverse a state supreme court decision allowing American Electric Power to charge data centers differently.

The Ohio Manufacturers’ Association is contesting a new utility billing structure that affects data centers, arguing that it could lead to increased operational costs for manufacturers. The association claims that the changes could disproportionately impact businesses that rely heavily on data services.
