151. A new era of utility wildfire defense is defined by a shift from reactive maintenance to proactive risk mitigation

Factor This™ | Tue, 23 Dec 2025 11:15:00 +0000

The Wildfire Risk Mitigation & Vegetation Management Symposium taking place at DTECH will bring together real-world utility use cases and the technology driving them.

Utilities are transitioning from reactive maintenance strategies to proactive risk mitigation in wildfire defense, aiming to enhance safety and reduce the impact of wildfires on power infrastructure. This shift involves implementing advanced technologies and strategies to identify and address potential wildfire risks before they escalate.
