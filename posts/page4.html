151. Eastern Shore group appeals Conowingo Dam certification

Factor This™ | Mon, 15 Dec 2025 20:40:01 +0000

A coalition from two Eastern Shore counties appealed Maryland's water quality certification for the Conowingo Dam, risking a $340 million settlement.

An Eastern Shore group has filed an appeal against the certification of the Conowingo Dam, citing concerns over its environmental impact. The appeal challenges the decision made by the Federal Energy Regulatory Commission (FERC) regarding the dam's operations and its effects on local ecosystems.
