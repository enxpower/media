Factor This™ | Mon, 12 Jan 2026 20:01:20 +0000

The Bureau of Reclamation released proposals for managing Colorado River reservoirs amid stalled negotiations among seven states over water sharing.

The federal government has released draft long-term plans for managing the Colorado River, addressing ongoing water shortages and the impacts of climate change. These plans aim to balance the needs of various stakeholders, including agricultural, municipal, and environmental interests, while ensuring sustainable water use in the region.
