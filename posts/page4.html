151. New analysis shows more US consumers are falling behind on their utility bills

Factor This™ | Tue, 02 Dec 2025 16:28:00 +0000

More consumers are falling behind on utility bills, with past due balances rising 9.7% to $789, according to The Century Foundation and Protect Borrowers.

A new analysis reveals that an increasing number of U.S. consumers are struggling to keep up with their utility bills, with many falling behind on payments. The report highlights the financial strain on households, exacerbated by rising energy costs and economic challenges.
