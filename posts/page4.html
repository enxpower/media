151. What to know about COP30, this year’s UN climate talks

Factor This™ | Mon, 10 Nov 2025 22:00:00 +0000

Delegates gather in Belem, Brazil, for COP30, aiming to address climate change amid concerns over participation and outcomes.

COP30, the 2023 UN climate talks, will take place in the United Arab Emirates from November 30 to December 12. The conference aims to advance global climate action and address key issues such as emissions reduction and climate finance. Attendees will include representatives from governments, NGOs, and the private sector, focusing on collaborative solutions to climate challenges.
