151. Two-decade hydropower plunge at big Colorado River dams

Factor This™ | Mon, 03 Nov 2025 18:35:00 +0000

Hydropower output at Hoover and Glen Canyon dams has significantly declined since 2000 due to reduced water levels in Lakes Mead and Powell.

Recent data shows that hydropower generation at major Colorado River dams has declined significantly over the past two decades. This drop is attributed to prolonged drought conditions and reduced water flow, impacting the energy output of these facilities.
