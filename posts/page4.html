A blackout hit the Dominican Republic on Tuesday, disrupting traffic and businesses, with 15% of power restored by night, authorities said.

The Dominican Republic experienced a full blackout, affecting the entire nation as power crews worked urgently to restore electricity. The outage has prompted a scramble to identify the causes and re-establish power across the country.
