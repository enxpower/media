151. Tax credit shifts put Texas renewables on a sprint to build

Factor This™ | Fri, 05 Sep 2025 11:00:00 +0000

At the Texas Clean Energy Summit, renewable energy developers weighed new federal tax guidance and supply chain pressures.

Texas renewable energy projects are accelerating their construction in response to recent shifts in tax credits. The changes are prompting developers to expedite their timelines to take advantage of the financial incentives before they potentially expire or change again.
