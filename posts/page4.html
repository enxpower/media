151. Ford to convert KY battery plant for energy storage business, reportedly laying off 1,500 workers

Factor This™ | Thu, 18 Dec 2025 19:57:53 +0000

Ford Motor Co. will lay off about 1,500 workers in Kentucky as it converts its Glendale plant from electric vehicle batteries to energy storage.

Ford is set to convert its Kentucky battery plant to focus on energy storage, which will reportedly result in the layoff of 1,500 workers. This shift is part of the company's strategy to pivot towards the growing energy storage market.
