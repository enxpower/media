Delegates gather in Belem, Brazil, for COP30, aiming to address climate change amid concerns over participation and outcomes.

COP30, the 2023 UN climate talks, will take place in Dubai from November 30 to December 12. The conference aims to advance global climate action and assess progress on the Paris Agreement, with a focus on reducing greenhouse gas emissions and enhancing climate resilience.
