151. Several counties drop out of challenge to $340 million Conowingo Dam settlement

Factor This™ | Mon, 05 Jan 2026 18:27:00 +0000

Cecil County continues its appeal against Maryland’s Conowingo Dam recertification, while other Eastern Shore counties have withdrawn, fearing delays.

Several counties have withdrawn from a legal challenge against a $340 million settlement related to the Conowingo Dam. The settlement aims to address environmental concerns and improve water quality in the Chesapeake Bay region.
