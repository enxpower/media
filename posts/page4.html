151. How can utilities improve their distribution planning for better resiliency and restoration?

Factor This™ | Thu, 08 Jan 2026 18:58:26 +0000

Contributor Jason Endsley asks the question: How do we build, maintain, and—when things go wrong—restore the system faster and more reliably with the resources we have?

Utilities can enhance their distribution planning by integrating advanced technologies and data analytics to improve resiliency and restoration efforts. This includes adopting smart grid technologies, conducting risk assessments, and engaging in proactive maintenance strategies to better prepare for and respond to outages.
