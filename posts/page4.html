151. Eastern Shore group appeals Conowingo Dam certification

Factor This™ | Mon, 15 Dec 2025 20:40:01 +0000

A coalition from two Eastern Shore counties appealed Maryland's water quality certification for the Conowingo Dam, risking a $340 million settlement.

An Eastern Shore group has filed an appeal against the certification of the Conowingo Dam, claiming that the decision undermines environmental protections. The appeal is part of ongoing concerns regarding the dam's impact on local ecosystems and water quality in the Chesapeake Bay area.
