151. Feds release draft long-term plans for Colorado River management

Factor This™ | Mon, 12 Jan 2026 20:01:20 +0000

The Bureau of Reclamation released proposals for managing Colorado River reservoirs amid stalled negotiations among seven states over water sharing.

The federal government has released draft long-term plans for managing the Colorado River, which is crucial for water supply in the southwestern United States. These plans aim to address ongoing drought conditions and water shortages affecting millions of residents and agricultural sectors in the region.
