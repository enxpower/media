151. Georgia Power to add 1 GW of solar through new PPAs

Factor This™ | Tue, 09 Sep 2025 18:51:07 +0000

Five projects approved by state regulators will expand the utility’s renewable portfolio, with more procurement rounds on the way.

Georgia Power has announced plans to add 1 gigawatt (GW) of solar energy capacity through new power purchase agreements (PPAs). This initiative is part of the company's strategy to expand its renewable energy portfolio and meet growing energy demands.
