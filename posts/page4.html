151. Why are Democrats suddenly downplaying climate change? – This Week in Cleantech

Factor This™ | Fri, 24 Oct 2025 16:02:28 +0000

In this edition of This Week in Cleantech, Kate Yoder from Grist discusses how Democrats are downplaying the term “climate change” after the 2024 election, following polling that shows voters prioritize affordability and health care over environmental issues.

Democrats are reportedly downplaying climate change as a political strategy, focusing on other pressing issues ahead of upcoming elections. This shift may reflect concerns about voter priorities and the effectiveness of climate policies in garnering support.
