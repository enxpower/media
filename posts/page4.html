151. What to know about COP30, this year’s UN climate talks

Factor This™ | Mon, 10 Nov 2025 22:00:00 +0000

Delegates gather in Belem, Brazil, for COP30, aiming to address climate change amid concerns over participation and outcomes.

COP30, the 30th annual UN climate talks, will take place in 2025 in Brazil. The conference aims to address global climate change issues and build upon the agreements made in previous COP meetings. Key topics will include emissions reduction, climate finance, and adaptation strategies.
