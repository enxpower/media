151. New Hampshire lawmakers renew push to restructure renewable energy quotas

Factor This™ | Thu, 13 Nov 2025 20:37:03 +0000

New Hampshire's House Finance Committee voted 14-11 on Oct. 30 to recommend changes to House Bill 219, affecting the Renewable Portfolio Standard.

New Hampshire lawmakers are advocating for a restructuring of the state's renewable energy quotas. This initiative aims to enhance the state's renewable energy goals and improve the overall energy landscape in New Hampshire.
