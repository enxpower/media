151. EPA funding salvaged in US Congress spending bill

Factor This™ | Tue, 06 Jan 2026 20:38:40 +0000

The EPA budget for 2026 rejects extreme cuts proposed by Republican representatives and the Trump administration. But advocates warn that significant cuts still jeopardize the agency’s ability to protect Americans’ health.

The U.S. Congress has included funding for the Environmental Protection Agency (EPA) in its latest spending bill, ensuring continued support for environmental programs. This decision comes after previous concerns about potential cuts to the agency's budget.
