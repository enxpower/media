151. CorPower Ocean to lead €30 million wave energy scaling project

Factor This™ | Mon, 15 Dec 2025 21:03:19 +0000

CorPower Ocean will lead the €30 million POWER-Farm EU Project to validate wave farm technology in UK waters, supported by Horizon Europe funding.

CorPower Ocean will lead a €30 million project aimed at scaling wave energy technology. The initiative is designed to enhance the efficiency and deployment of wave energy systems across Europe.
