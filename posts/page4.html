151. Supply-chain delays, rising equipment prices threaten electricity grid

Factor This™ | Wed, 19 Nov 2025 23:03:01 +0000

Severe equipment shortages and years-long lead times are slowing grid upgrades at the same time U.S. power demand is rising sharply.

Supply-chain delays and increasing equipment prices are posing significant threats to the electricity grid, as these factors can hinder the timely deployment of renewable energy projects. The rising costs and logistical challenges are impacting the overall efficiency and reliability of energy supply systems.
