151. Rare win for renewable energy: Trump administration funds geothermal network expansion

Factor This™ | Thu, 04 Dec 2025 18:26:00 +0000

The U.S. Department of Energy approved an $8.6 million grant for Eversource Energy to expand its geothermal network in Framingham, Massachusetts.

The Trump administration has announced funding for the expansion of a geothermal energy network, marking a significant development for renewable energy initiatives. This funding aims to enhance geothermal resources and improve energy access in various regions.
