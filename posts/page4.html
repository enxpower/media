151. Eastern Shore group appeals Conowingo Dam certification

Factor This™ | Mon, 15 Dec 2025 20:40:01 +0000

A coalition from two Eastern Shore counties appealed Maryland's water quality certification for the Conowingo Dam, risking a $340 million settlement.

An Eastern Shore group has filed an appeal against the certification of the Conowingo Dam, arguing that the dam's operation poses environmental risks. The appeal seeks to challenge the decision made by the Maryland Department of the Environment, which certified the dam's compliance with state regulations.
