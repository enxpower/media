National Hydropower Association President Malcolm Woolf issues strong support for Energy Secretary Chris Wright's directive to streamline hydropower licensing with FERC.

The National Hydropower Association (NHA) has praised the Department of Energy's (DOE) decision to protect hydropower permits. This move is expected to enhance the development and sustainability of hydropower projects in the United States.
