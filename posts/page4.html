151. Brookfield says hydro and nuclear are back in demand as baseload PPAs shift to firm power

Factor This™ | Mon, 10 Nov 2025 19:06:31 +0000

Firm power needs are reshaping Brookfield’s strategy as data center growth pushes utilities and developers toward hydro upgrades, long-term storage contracts and a nuclear buildout backed by the U.S. government.

Brookfield Renewable Partners has reported an increased demand for hydro and nuclear energy as the market shifts from baseload Power Purchase Agreements (PPAs) to firm power contracts. This transition reflects a growing recognition of the reliability and stability that these energy sources provide in meeting energy needs.
