151. Understanding the benefits of Salt River Project’s shift from webTrans to webTrans AFC with Derek Sicher

Factor This™ | Mon, 22 Dec 2025 19:17:29 +0000

At the 2025 OATI Energy Conference, Derek Sicher, Senior Project Manager – IT at Salt River Project (SRP) sat down with John Engel from OATI to discuss ever evolving customer needs and how SRP is managing congestion and the transmission system.

Salt River Project (SRP) is transitioning from its webTrans platform to webTrans AFC, aiming to enhance operational efficiency and improve data management. Derek Sicher discusses the benefits of this shift, which include better integration of data and streamlined processes for energy management. The move is part of SRP's broader strategy to modernize its grid and support renewable energy initiatives.
