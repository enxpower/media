151. What to know about COP30, this year’s UN climate talks

Factor This™ | Mon, 10 Nov 2025 22:00:00 +0000

Delegates gather in Belem, Brazil, for COP30, aiming to address climate change amid concerns over participation and outcomes.

COP30, the 30th annual UN climate talks, will take place in 2025 in Brazil, focusing on global climate action and commitments. The conference aims to address critical issues such as emissions reduction, climate finance, and adaptation strategies to combat climate change effects. Participants will include representatives from various countries, NGOs, and stakeholders in the climate sector.
