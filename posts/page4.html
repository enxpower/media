151. Exploring the evolution of OG&E’s grid to best meet increasing demand of the past and in the future with Ryan Jones

Factor This™ | Mon, 22 Dec 2025 19:17:25 +0000

At the 2025 OATI Energy Conference, Ryan Jones, Grid Innovation Manager at OG&E sat down with John Engel from OATI to talk about the transformation that OG&E is going through on every level, highlighting the positive impact these developments have made on Oklahoma region's customers and economy.

Ryan Jones discusses the evolution of OG&E's grid to address the increasing energy demand both historically and in the future. The focus is on modernization efforts to enhance grid reliability and efficiency in response to growing consumption patterns.
