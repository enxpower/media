151. Conowingo Dam appeal dropped, allowing $340M settlement to go forward

Factor This™ | Fri, 09 Jan 2026 18:38:00 +0000

Maryland's $340 million settlement with Constellation for the Conowingo Dam can proceed after Eastern Shore counties dropped their appeal.

The appeal regarding the Conowingo Dam has been dropped, enabling a $340 million settlement to proceed. This settlement is part of an agreement aimed at addressing environmental concerns related to the dam's operations.
