151. RI, CT attorneys general move to join forces with Revolution Wind developers in case against feds

Factor This™ | Tue, 06 Jan 2026 19:43:00 +0000

Rhode Island and Connecticut attorneys general filed to consolidate their lawsuit against the Trump administration over the Revolution Wind project.

The attorneys general of Rhode Island and Connecticut are collaborating with Revolution Wind developers to challenge federal actions that they believe could hinder the offshore wind project. This partnership aims to protect the interests of the states and the developers in the face of federal regulatory decisions.
