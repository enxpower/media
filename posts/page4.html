151. Los Angeles officially exits coal

Factor This™ | Mon, 08 Dec 2025 20:50:00 +0000

Los Angeles, led by Mayor Karen Bass, has fully divested from coal in its power supply to transition to 100% clean energy by 2035, utilizing new hydrogen-capable units at the IPP facility.

Los Angeles has officially exited coal as a source of energy, marking a significant milestone in its transition to cleaner energy sources. The city has committed to achieving 100% renewable energy by 2035, with the closure of its last coal-fired power plant contributing to this goal.
