151. CPUC approves ‘first-ever’ statewide energy efficiency market transformation initiatives

Factor This™ | Tue, 09 Dec 2025 15:52:00 +0000

The California Public Utilities Commission approved statewide energy efficiency Market Transformation Initiatives to promote energy-efficient products, benefiting ratepayers.

The California Public Utilities Commission (CPUC) has approved the first-ever statewide energy efficiency market transformation initiatives aimed at reducing energy consumption across the state. These initiatives are designed to promote innovative solutions and technologies that enhance energy efficiency in various sectors, contributing to California's climate goals.
