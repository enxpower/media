151. Can new hydro development help Tajikistan’s VMKB province meet rising demand?

The Hydropower Sustainability Alliance presented the Power System Plan 2025-2050 for VMKB province in Tajikistan at a ministerial meeting.

Tajikistan's VMKB province is exploring new hydroelectric development to address its increasing energy demand. The region's reliance on hydropower makes it crucial to enhance capacity to meet the needs of its growing population and economy.
