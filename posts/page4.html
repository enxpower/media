151. Regulator approves electricity transmission line, towers in Virginia to serve one data center

Factor This™ | Thu, 21 Aug 2025 17:55:42 +0000

The State Corporation Commission approved new high-voltage project and rejected complaints from homeowners that the substation will be too close to the Bren Pointe community.

Virginia regulators have approved the construction of a new electricity transmission line and towers to supply power to a single data center. This decision is part of efforts to enhance the state's energy infrastructure to meet the growing demands of data facilities.
