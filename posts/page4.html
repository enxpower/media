151. SECC and PLMA partner up to put customers at the center of utility DER strategies

Factor This™ | Mon, 20 Oct 2025 14:13:16 +0000

PLMA and SECC are newly collaborating to offer a combined one-day event focused on the critical intersection of customer engagement and distributed energy resources (DERs).

The Smart Electric Power Alliance (SECC) and the Peak Load Management Alliance (PLMA) have formed a partnership aimed at enhancing utility demand-side management strategies by focusing on customer needs. This collaboration will explore innovative approaches to integrate distributed energy resources (DER) into utility operations, ensuring that customer preferences are prioritized in the development of these strategies.
