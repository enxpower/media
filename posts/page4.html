151. Transmission oversight gives project managers ‘eyes in the field’

Factor This™ | Tue, 09 Dec 2025 18:32:20 +0000

Extra eyes in the field will be necessary along with other resources if the current speed of transmission construction is to keep pace with experts’ projections, writes contributor Bill Perry.

The article discusses how enhanced transmission oversight allows project managers to have better visibility and control over energy projects in the field. This oversight is crucial for ensuring efficiency and addressing issues promptly during the development and operation of transmission systems.
