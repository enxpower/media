151. New plan for Colorado River is still murky as states pass by early deadline

Factor This™ | Mon, 08 Dec 2025 15:41:00 +0000

Utah and six states are negotiating water sharing for the Colorado River, with a deadline set for February, but no firm agreement yet.

The deadline for states to submit their proposals for a new Colorado River water management plan has passed, but many states have yet to reach an agreement. The ongoing negotiations are complicated by differing water needs and priorities among the states involved, leaving the future of the river's management uncertain.
