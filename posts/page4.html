151. Solar, batteries push down natural gas generation in California, EIA says

Factor This™ | Tue, 25 Nov 2025 17:33:30 +0000

New EIA data shows natural gas output falling 17 percent year-over-year as utility-scale solar and battery storage reshape California’s grid.

According to the U.S. Energy Information Administration (EIA), solar energy and battery storage are significantly reducing natural gas generation in California. The increased use of renewable energy sources has led to a decline in reliance on natural gas for electricity generation in the state.
