151. Transmission oversight gives project managers ‘eyes in the field’

Extra eyes in the field will be necessary along with other resources if the current speed of transmission construction is to keep pace with experts’ projections, writes contributor Bill Perry.

Project managers in the energy sector are utilizing enhanced transmission oversight tools that provide real-time data and insights, allowing them to monitor projects more effectively. This technology enables better decision-making and improves the management of energy transmission systems.
