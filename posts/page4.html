151. Safeguarding reliability during the migration from TDM to packet-based systems

Factor This™ | Thu, 08 Jan 2026 18:35:57 +0000

The utility industry is facing a pivotal moment: the legacy platforms that enable time-division multiplexing (TDM) are reaching end-of-life. These systems have become increasingly unsustainable due to rising costs and vendor support issues, and they can’t support advanced applications like IEC 61850 automation, advanced cybersecurity, smart devices or the integration of distributed energy resource

The article discusses the transition from Time Division Multiplexing (TDM) to packet-based systems in power grids, emphasizing the importance of maintaining reliability during this migration. It highlights the challenges and strategies involved in ensuring that the shift does not compromise the stability and efficiency of energy distribution systems.
