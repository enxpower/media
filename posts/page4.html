151. How can solar EPCs navigate a complex market?

Factor This™ | Thu, 23 Oct 2025 19:03:43 +0000

EPC companies face diverse risks in solar projects, requiring financing certainty and compliance with domestic content regulations to succeed, writes contributor Brandon Childers, President, IGS Solar.

Solar EPCs (Engineering, Procurement, and Construction) are facing challenges in a complex market characterized by fluctuating demand, regulatory changes, and technological advancements. To navigate these complexities, EPCs must adopt flexible strategies, enhance their project management capabilities, and stay informed about market trends and policies.
