151. Understanding the benefits of Salt River Project’s shift from webTrans to webTrans AFC with Derek Sicher

Factor This™ | Mon, 22 Dec 2025 19:17:29 +0000

At the 2025 OATI Energy Conference, Derek Sicher, Senior Project Manager – IT at Salt River Project (SRP) sat down with John Engel from OATI to discuss ever evolving customer needs and how SRP is managing congestion and the transmission system.

Salt River Project (SRP) is transitioning from its existing webTrans system to the advanced webTrans AFC platform to enhance operational efficiency and data management. This shift aims to provide improved functionalities for grid management and better support for renewable energy integration. Derek Sicher discusses the benefits of this upgrade, emphasizing its role in modernizing SRP's infrastructure.
