151. Renewable jobs lagging behind clean energy deployment

Factor This™ | Thu, 15 Jan 2026 16:07:00 +0000

A report from International Labor Organization and IRENA says the ‘human side’ of the energy transition is being overlooked.

A recent report indicates that job growth in the renewable energy sector is not keeping pace with the rapid deployment of clean energy technologies. Despite significant investments and advancements in renewable energy, the workforce expansion has been slower than expected, raising concerns about the industry's ability to meet future demands.
