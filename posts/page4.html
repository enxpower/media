151. Solar growth cushions Colorado River hydropower declines

Factor This™ | Mon, 27 Oct 2025 17:24:00 +0000

Arizona water policy officials met in Phoenix to discuss extending a drought emergency due to low reservoirs and minimal precipitation.

The growth of solar energy in the southwestern United States is helping to offset declines in hydropower production from the Colorado River. As climate change and prolonged drought impact water levels, solar installations are increasing, providing a crucial alternative energy source.
