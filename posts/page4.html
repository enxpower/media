151. Ukraine’s state-owned hydro company lands €75 million EBRD loan to restore plants damaged in war

Factor This™ | Mon, 12 Jan 2026 20:22:41 +0000

The European Bank for Reconstruction and Development and PJSC Ukrhydroenergo signed a €75 million loan for hydropower modernization in Ukraine.

Ukraine's state-owned hydro company has secured a €75 million loan from the European Bank for Reconstruction and Development (EBRD) to restore hydro plants that were damaged during the ongoing war. This funding aims to support the rehabilitation of critical energy infrastructure in the country.
