151. Amid tense negotiations over the Colorado River’s future, Arizona mayors unite against ‘threat’ to state’s water

Factor This™ | Mon, 08 Sep 2025 19:56:11 +0000

The Central Arizona Project and over 20 Arizona cities are forming a new coalition to advocate for their water on the Colorado River, saying some other states are “unwilling” to do what is necessary to protect it.

Arizona mayors have come together to oppose what they describe as a 'threat' to the state's water supply amid ongoing negotiations regarding the future of the Colorado River. The mayors are concerned about potential agreements that could negatively impact Arizona's water resources.
