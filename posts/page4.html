151. Eastern Shore group appeals Conowingo Dam certification

Factor This™ | Mon, 15 Dec 2025 20:40:01 +0000

A coalition from two Eastern Shore counties appealed Maryland's water quality certification for the Conowingo Dam, risking a $340 million settlement.

An Eastern Shore group has filed an appeal against the certification of the Conowingo Dam, arguing that the dam's operations negatively impact local water quality and aquatic life. The appeal seeks to challenge the regulatory approval granted to the dam, which is a significant hydroelectric facility on the Susquehanna River.
