151. How are DER flexibility markets transforming the grid and enabling utility savings?

Factor This™ | Mon, 15 Dec 2025 20:50:45 +0000

Transforming the grid to meet skyrocketing energy demand isn't just about switching generation sources.

Distributed Energy Resource (DER) flexibility markets are reshaping the energy grid by allowing utilities to manage demand and supply more efficiently. These markets enable utilities to save costs by utilizing flexible resources, which can respond to changes in energy demand and generation. As a result, DER flexibility markets are playing a crucial role in enhancing grid reliability and supporting the transition to renewable energy sources.
